To design BCD to Decimal decoder using IC-7442





  1. Make connections as per the circuit diagram and pin diagram of IC.
  2. Click on Check Connections button. If connections are right, click on ‘OK’, then Simulation will become active.
  3. Provide the input by clicking toggle switches A0, A1, A2 and A3.
  4. Fill the observed values in the Truth Table.
  5. Verify Truth Table by clicking on Check button, if outputs are correct then click on OK.
  6. Click on the Reset button to reset the page.
